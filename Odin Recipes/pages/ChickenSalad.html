Lazy Chicken Bowl Salad

Why it's great:

Very filling high protein.

Easy to make but still very tasty.

ingredients

  • cooked chicken breast store bought or leftover
  • mixed salad greens.
  • cherry tomatoes
  • cucumber
  • olive oil or vinaigrette
  • salt & pepper

Steps

  1. Slice Chicken.
  2. Throw greens, tomatoes, and cucumber in a bowl.
  3. Add chicken.
  4. Drizzle olive oil or dressing.

Time:5-7 minutes
