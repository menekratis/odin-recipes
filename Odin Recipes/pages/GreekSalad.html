Greek Salad

Why it's Great

No Cooking. Just chop and mix.

The Secret is picking the right ingredients!

ingredients

  • 2-3 Tomatoes
  • 1 Cucumber
  • 1/2 red onion
  • 80-100g feta cheese
  • Handful of olives
  • Olive oil
  • oregano
  • salt

Steps

  1. Wash your tomatoes and cucumber with clean water, and have a bowl.
  2. Chop tomatoes, cucumber, and onion.
  3. Add olives and feta on top
  4. Drizzle olive oil.
  5. Sprinkle oregano and a little salt.
