Lazy Ceasar's salad

Why it's great:

Still tasty but simpler than restaurant versions. Easy to make but still very tasty.

ingredients

  • Romaine lettuce
  • Parmesan
  • Croutons
  • Caesar dressing
  • olive oil or vinaigrette
  • salt & pepper

Steps

  1. Chop lettuce
  2. Toss caesar dressing and mix.
  3. Add parmesan and croutons.
  4. Optional cut pieces of grilled chicken

Time: 5 minutes
